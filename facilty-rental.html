University of Miami faculty and staff are welcome to host their events with us. With a seating capacity for 450 guests and state-of-the-art acoustics and technology infrastructure, the Center supports a full range of lectures, panel discussions, and musical and theatrical performances. The Kislak Center sparks a heightened and mutually sustaining dynamic between University of Miami intellectual and cultural programs and the communities of South Florida.

For venue availability and questions about reserving the Center to host your event, please contact Ivette Uria.
